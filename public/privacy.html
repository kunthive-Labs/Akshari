Privacy notice

Last updated: July 18, 2026

Data we process

Fontscape does not require accounts in v1. The service processes search terms, technical request metadata, and operational logs to provide and protect the service.

Retention

Operational logs are retained only for the period needed to diagnose reliability and security issues. Search terms are not sold or used for advertising.

Cookies

Fontscape does not set advertising cookies. A local browser preference may store your selected appearance theme.

Self-hosting

Self-hosted operators control their own logs, backups, deployment configuration, and data retention. Review this notice and adapt it to your deployment.

Contact

For privacy requests, contact the maintainer through the project repository.
